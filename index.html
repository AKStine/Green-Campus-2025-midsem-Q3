Join us in making USIU–Africa sustainable, green, and future-ready.

Why Go Green?

  • Reduce campus waste and energy use
  • Promote tree planting and biodiversity
  • Encourage water conservation
  • Support eco-friendly transport

Every student action counts! Click "Join the Campaign" to learn how you can help.

About Green Campus 2025

Green Campus 2025 is USIU–Africa’s commitment to sustainability. Our goal: carbon-neutral campus by 2025!

How you can participate:

  • Join campus clean-up days
  • Use refillable bottles and reduce plastic
  • Cycle or walk to class
  • Take part in tree planting events
